Bacon

Description

This is a super easy recipe for amazing crispy bacon. Super simple, super fast, and of course super tasty! Enjoy the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 6 rashers streaky bacon

Steps

  1. Lay the bacon on the base of an air fryer.
  2. Set the air fryer to 200 C and cook for 8 mins.
  3. Remove the bacon and drain of paper towel before serving. Enjoy!
