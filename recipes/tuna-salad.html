Tuna Salad Sandwich

Description

This is a super easy recipe for yummy tuna salad sandwiches. Super simple, super fast, and of course super tasty! Enjoy the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 145 g tuna
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 8 slices cucumber

Steps

  1. Spread the tuna onto one slice of bread.
  2. Spread the mayonnaise on the other slice of bread.
  3. Add the cucumbers before pressing the two slices together.
