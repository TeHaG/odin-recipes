Pancakes

Description

This is a super easy recipe for gorgeous dutch pancakes. Super simple, super fast, and of course super tasty! Enjoy the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Whisk together flour and eggs.
  2. Add milk slowly and mix thoroughly.
  3. Grease frying pan and pour 1/4 cup of batter into pan. Cook on medium heat till bubbles form on surface. Flip pancake and continue to cook for a minute or two. Remove and serve with butter and syrup. Enjoy!
